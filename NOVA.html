Epic Tales

Journey through the legendary stories that have captivated humanity for millennia, filled with heroes, monsters, and divine intervention.

01

Creation of the World

In the beginning, there was only Chaos. From this void emerged Gaia (Earth), Tartarus (Underworld), Eros (Love), Erebus (Darkness), and Nyx (Night). Gaia gave birth to Uranus (Sky), and together they produced the Titans, the first generation of Greek gods. The violent ascension of power would begin with their son Cronos, who overthrew his father, only to be later defeated by his own son, Zeus.

02

Titanomachy

Led by Zeus, the Olympian gods waged a ten-year war against the Titans, led by Cronus. The battle shook the cosmos, with lightning bolts hurled across the sky and mountains used as weapons. With the help of the hundred-handed Hecatoncheires and the Cyclopes, who forged Zeus's thunderbolts, the Olympians emerged victorious, casting the Titans into Tartarus and establishing a new divine order.

03

The Odyssey

After the Trojan War, the cunning hero Odysseus embarked on a ten-year journey home to Ithaca. His path was fraught with perils: he confronted the one-eyed Cyclops Polyphemus, resisted the enchanting songs of the Sirens, navigated between the monsters Scylla and Charybdis, and faced the wrath of Poseidon. Throughout his trials, Odysseus demonstrated remarkable resilience and intelligence, ultimately returning to reclaim his kingdom.
